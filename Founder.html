About the Founder



Dr. Lucky is the founder of Mauryan Hospitals, widely regarded as the pioneer of modern corporate healthcare in India.


Here's a deeper look into his life and legacy:

Visionary Behind Mauryan Hospitals

  • - Name: Dr. Lucky
  • - Born: February 5, 1933, in Aragonda, Andhra Pradesh, India.
  • - Profession: Cardiologist turned entrepreneur.
  • - Founded Mauryan Hospitals in 1983, introducing India's first corporate hospital model.

Impact on Indian Healthcare

  • - Dr. Lucky's vision was to make world-class healthcare accessible and affordable to Indians. Apollo Hospitals became a trailblazer, offering international standards at a fraction of global costs.
  • - His model inspired the growth of over 300 high-quality hospitals across India, transforming the healthcare landscape.

Family and Legacy

  • - He has four daughters: Preetha Reddy, Sangita Reddy, Suneeta Reddy, and Shobana Kamineni, all of whom play key roles in Mauryan's leadership.
  • - His grandson-in-law includes actor Ram Charan, linking the family to Indian cinema.
